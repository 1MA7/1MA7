Core Methodologies

My work is grounded in robust, evidence-based methodologies. The following approaches are central to my process of transforming data into actionable insights and sustainable impact, connecting technical rigor with real-world application.

Outcome Harvesting

Identifies and validates non-predefined behavioral changes to understand emergent, real-world impact in complex environments.

Quasi-Experimental Analysis

Uses "with and without project" scenarios to isolate and measure the specific impact of an intervention, providing a rigorous basis for evaluation.

DAC Evaluation Criteria

Applies standardized criteria (Relevance, Effectiveness, Impact, etc.) for objective, comprehensive project assessment.

Knowledge Management

Structures a complete data-to-learning lifecycle, ensuring insights are captured, shared, and used for organizational improvement.

Synthesized Impact
